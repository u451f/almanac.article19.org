Privacy Community Group | Privacy CG

Privacy CG aims to develop web features and APIs that improve user privacy through browser enhancements. The design of these features impact how cookies and browsing information is stored and shared. Security vulnerabilities may allow malicious actors to obtain a user's session cookies to impersonate the user, perform actions on behalf of the user, or gain access to sensitive information.

  • Current drafts: Cookies Having Independent Partitioned State prevents third-party cookies from linking the users across different sites while functioning within one specific site.
