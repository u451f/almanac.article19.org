Guess who! - Who are the most dominant actors in setting standards?

This is a visual map for those who are curious about which actors are dominating the standardisation of internet infrastructure. This map aims to answer three questions:

  • Which actors are most dominant in formal leadership positions of groups and committees within the Internet Standards Almanac?
  • Which actors are most dominant in authoring published technical standards in groups and committees identified in the Internet Standards Almanac?
  • Which actors are most dominant in discussions in groups and committees identified in the Internet Standards Almanac?

This map aims to help you visualise the dominance of particular actors across internet-related SDOs with an overview of each SDO as well as a granular level within particular groups and committees. Feel free to toggle around to see how dominance has changed over the years.

The raw data used to generate these visualisations may be accessed here.
