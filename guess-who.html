Who are the most dominant actors in setting standards?

This is a visual map for those who are curious about which actors dominate the standardisation of internet infrastructure. This map aims to answer three questions: Read moreâ€¦

  • Who leads? Which actors dominate formal leadership positions of groups and committees within the Internet Standards Almanac?
  • Who speaks? Which actors dominate discussions in groups and committees identified in the Internet Standards Almanac?
  • Who publishes? Which actors publish the most technical standards in groups and committees identified in the Internet Standards Almanac?

This map aims to help you see which actors hold the most power in setting standards across internet-related standards developing organisations (SDOs). It contains an overview of each SDO as well as a granular list of working groups and committees within that SDO. Feel free to toggle around to see how dominance has changed over the years.
