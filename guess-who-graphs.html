These graphs aim to help you see which actors hold the most power in setting standards across internet-related standards developing organisations (SDOs) over time. They contain a granular list of working groups and committees within selected SDOs. Feel free to toggle around to see how dominance has changed over the years or click on a specific actor to isolate and monitor their activity over time.
