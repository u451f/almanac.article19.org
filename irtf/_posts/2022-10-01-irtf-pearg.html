Privacy Enhancements and Assessments Research Group | PEARG

PEARG aims to convene participants to discuss and review the use and applicability of new privacy-enhancing technologies and mechanisms. It also documents existing research on new and existing privacy assessment methodologies to inform future development of protocol specifications.

  • Standard under development: A Survey of Worldwide Censorship Techniques is an informational document that describes the technical mechanisms that state actors employ to block and censor internet traffic.
